What is this?

Encode messages with a simple cipher and decode them back. Perfect for fun, puzzles, or learning the basics of secret codes!

Why were ciphers used?

  • 🛡️ Julius Caesar used them for secret military messages.
  • 📜 One of the earliest encryption methods.
  • 🎓 Today, mainly educational or fun.

Quick Example

Original: HELLO WORLD

Encoded (Shift 3): KHOOR ZRUOG

Decoded: HELLO WORLD

Features

  • 🔐 Encode your secret messages.
  • 🔓 Decode messages back to original.
  • 📋 Copy/share your secrets.
  • ⚙️ Adjustable shift value.

How does it work?

This tool uses the Caesar Cipher method:

  • Each letter is shifted by a number (called Shift Value).
  • Example: Shift = 3 → A → D, B → E, etc.
  • Decoding reverses the process.
